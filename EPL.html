OUR FEES

We apply two kinds of standard fees to our payment plan. The one-time set-up fee which is the cost of entering into start the installment plan. Then we have a monthly admin fee that accompanies each instalment payment.

We also apply a late payment fee - please read our terms and conditions for more information.

CREDIT CHECK

We use Payment Services Directive (PSD2) to do a credit check. When you click “Apply for EBS Pay Later” you will need to authorise us to access information from your bank. This information will help us determine if you are eligible for EBS Pay Later. Some of the information we use might include transaction history. Your privacy is our highest priority and we will not save, share, or disclose this information.

The credit check will take approximately 1 minute and you will get an instant answer if you are eligible or not.

PAYMENTS

You will make the first payment using your debit or credit card once you are approved. After this we will conveniently make automatic recurring payments every 30 days from using the card you provided to us.
